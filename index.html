    MTH308

    This is a set of lecture notes for the module "Cartesian tensors and mathematical models of elastic solids and viscous fluids" taught at XJTLU in 2014 and 2015. The module is part of the Applied Mathematics programme (year 4). The notes are supposed to be self-contained but assume a working knowledge of calculus, linear algebra and classical mechanics
